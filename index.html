What is Hard-Desk Expo...??

“The reality is that no good design can actually be realized without the use of functional hardware; after all what is a piece of furniture without fittings – mere planks of wood!” –by Md Faiz

Hardex-Expo is a Computer Hardware Exhibition organised by Computer Application Department in Sachidanand Sinha College Aurangabad(Bihar) and a serious platform for the students in the computer hardware components to showcase their projects. It is the only focused and the hardware exhibition in this college, with participation from all the BCA first year(2018-2021) students. As we know that most of the students only know the theoretical parts of the hardware system.Very view of the students had seen the internal components of Computer hardware.So by keeping this point in mind the Computer Application Department has organised the Computer Hardware Exhibition among the students of BCA first year so that they practically see and touch the various Hardware Components and know all the functions of the hardware not only theoretically but even also seen the internal parts with their naked eyes. When it comes to choosing the best exhibition in Sachidanand Sinha College,are Hard-Desk Expo-2019 is second to none.

Some of the renowned projects who have been present at the Hard-Desk Expo-2019 show in Sachidanand Sinha College, Motherboard, CPU, RAM/ROM, Laptop, SMPS, Wire and Wiring, BIOS, etc.

What is Computer HARDWARE..??

Computer hardware is a collective term used to describe any of the physical components of an analog or digital computer. The term hardware distinguishes the tangible aspects of a computing device from software, which consists of written instructions that tell physical components what to do.

Computer Hardware can be categorizeed as having either internal or external components.
Internal components:It include items such as the motherboard, central processing unit (CPU), random access memory (RAM), hard drive, optical drive, heat sink, power supply, transistors, chips, graphics processing unit (GPU), and network interface card (NIC). Theses components collectively process or store the instructions delivered by the program or operating system (OS).
External components:It is also called peripheral components, are those items that are often connected to the computer in order to control either its input or output. Common input components include a mouse, keyboard, microphone, camera, touch pad , stylus, joystick, scanner, USB flash drive or memory card. Monitors, printers, speakers, headphones and earphones/earbuds are all examples of output computer hardware components. All of these hardware devices are designed to either provide instructions to the software or render results from its execution.
